# 📊 AI Job Market Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes AI job market trends, salary insights, skill demand, and employment patterns.

The dashboard helps users understand:

* AI hiring trends
* Salary distribution
* Highest paying AI roles
* In-demand skills
* Employment type analysis
* Company size impact on salaries

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Power BI
* DAX
* Excel
* Data Visualization

---

## 📈 Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: AI Job Market Intelligence Dashboard

#### KPIs

* Total Jobs
* Average Salary
* Total Companies
* Total Open Positions

#### Visuals

* Jobs by Location
* Employment Type Distribution
* AI Job Opportunities by Industry
* Average Salary by Job Role

---

### Page 2: Salary Analysis Dashboard

#### KPIs

* Total Industries
* Max Salary
* Min Salary
* Salary Range

#### Visuals

* Average Salary by Employment Type
* Highest Paying Skills
* Top 10 Highest Paying AI Job Roles
* Average Salary by Company Size

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* AI Engineers and AI Researchers are among the highest-paid roles.
* Specialized AI skills command premium salaries.
* Company size influences compensation levels.
* Employment type impacts salary trends.
